Verifiable Secret Sharing (VSS)

Without trusting the dealer: every share can be verified without learning the secret. Cheating shares are caught.

Offline · no server · your secret never leaves this tab

    Commitments (public — share these for verification)

    Keep the shares secret, share the commitments openly. Shareholders can check their share under "Verify my share".

    Reconstructed secret

    With classic Shamir you have to trust the dealer who creates the shares. Feldman VSS removes that: the dealer publishes public commitments to the coefficients.

    • Commitment C_j = g^(a_j) mod p — reveals nothing about the secret (the discrete log is hard).
    • Verification: if g^share == Π C_j^(x^j) mod p holds, the share is honest.
    • If it fails, the share is corrupted or the dealer cheated — caught immediately.

    Group: RFC 3526 2048-bit safe prime. The secret is encoded as a single integer in Z_q (at most ~254 bytes) and recovered by Lagrange interpolation.

    All computation runs inside your browser (BigInt); nothing is sent anywhere. The share/commitment format matches vss.py exactly. For educational use.
